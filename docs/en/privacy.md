# Privacy Policy for AI Clash

Last updated: April 24, 2026

## 1. Data Collection

AI Clash mainly runs in your browser. For web-based channels, conversations usually happen directly between your browser and the respective AI service providers, such as DeepSeek, Doubao, Qwen, Tencent Yuanbao, Wenxin Yiyan, and Xiaomi MIMO.

When you use the built-in summary service, AI Clash sends the original question and the selected channel responses to the official AI Clash summary service at `https://ai-clash-service.snewbie.site` to generate the summary. This content is used only to process the requested summary. It is not used to train models and is not shared with third parties by us.

If you do not want summary content to be sent to the official AI Clash summary service, you can turn off automatic summary or configure your own API key as the summary provider.

## 2. Permissions Use

- **Tabs/ActiveTab**: Used to synchronize and switch between different AI provider tabs for a seamless experience.
- **Storage**: Used locally to save your preferences, such as enabled models and custom prompts.
- **Scripting/Content Scripts**: Used to interact with the web interface of AI providers you have already logged into.
- **Host permissions**: Used to access supported AI websites, supported API endpoints, and the official AI Clash summary service when you use the built-in summary feature.

## 3. Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with third-party AI websites and API services. Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## 4. Contact

If you have questions, please contact us via GitHub Issues.
