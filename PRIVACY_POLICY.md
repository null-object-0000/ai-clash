# Privacy Policy for AI Clash (AI 对撞机)

Last updated: April 15, 2026

## 1. Data Collection
AI Clash is a browser-side tool. All AI model interactions happen directly between your browser and the respective AI service providers (e.g., DeepSeek, Kimi, etc.). We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your conversations, prompts, or personal data to our own servers.

## 2. Permissions Use
- **Tabs/ActiveTab**: Used to synchronize and switch between different AI provider tabs for a seamless experience.
- **Storage**: Used locally to save your preferences, such as enabled models and custom prompts.
- **Scripting/Content Scripts**: Used to interact with the web interface of AI providers you have already logged into.

## 3. Third-Party Services
This extension interacts with third-party AI websites. Your use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## 4. Contact
If you have questions, please contact us via GitHub Issues.
